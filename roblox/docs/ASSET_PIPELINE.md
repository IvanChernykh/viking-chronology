# Asset Pipeline and Licensing

## Intake record

Every mesh, texture, animation, sound and font must record:

- source URL or internal asset owner;
- license name and version;
- author/attribution requirements;
- modification permission;
- commercial-use permission;
- Roblox asset ID after upload;
- optimization owner and review date.

## 3D pipeline

1. Source or model at consistent metric scale.
2. Historical silhouette/material review.
3. Retopology and UV review.
4. Shared trim/atlas assignment.
5. Collision proxy and interaction proxy.
6. LOD0/LOD1/LOD2 or engine-compatible simplification.
7. Export with pivots and naming convention.
8. Studio material/lighting review.
9. Mobile memory and overdraw test.

## Prohibited

- unverified marketplace rips;
- assets without redistribution/commercial permission;
- copied game files;
- hidden scripts in imported models;
- direct use of high-poly scans without optimization;
- AI-generated historical claims presented as evidence.
